# Magical Athlete: Official Game Manual

Magical Athlete is a racing game of pure chaos!

## Objective
The game is played over four races. The goal is to have the most points after all four races have concluded.

## Setup
1. **Preparation**: Place the racetrack in the center of the table (starting with the "Mild Mile" side up).
2. **Point Chips**: Place the point chips within reach. Stack the gold (1st place) and silver (2nd place) chips in order, with the largest on the bottom.
3. **Drafting**: To recruit a team of four racers:
   - Flip over twice as many racer cards as there are players (e.g., 6 cards for 3 players, 8 for 4, 10 for 5, and 12 for 6).
   - Players roll their die to determine the draft order (highest rolls first).
   - It is a "snake draft": players pick clockwise until the last player picks, then the order reverses counter-clockwise until everyone has two racers.
   - This process is repeated once more so that every player has a total of four racers.
4. **Before the Race**: 
   - Players roll to see who goes first in the first race.
   - Every player simultaneously chooses a racer card from their hand and reveals them at the same time. 
   - Place all used racer tokens on the "Start" space.

## Gameplay

### Turn Structure
On your turn, roll your die and move your racer that number of spaces. This is your "main move".

### Abilities
All racers have unique, often game-breaking abilities. If a power says you CAN use it, it is optional; all other powers are mandatory.

### Triggering Powers
If multiple powers trigger simultaneously, they activate in this priority order:
1. Current Player
2. Other Players (in clockwise order)
3. Racetrack Spaces

### Race End
The race ends immediately when the second racer crosses the finish line. The first racer to cross takes the 1st place gold chip, and the second takes the 2nd place silver chip.

### Between Races
After the race, put the used cards and tokens out of play. Flip the track to the other side (alternate between the "Mild" and "Wild" sides each race).

## Key Terminology

- **Main Move**: The action taken on your turn when you roll the die and move that many spaces.
- **Trip**: A tripped racer lies face down and skips their next main move.
- **Warp**: When a racer warps, they move their token to a new space without it counting as "moving" for the purposes of triggering other powers.
- **Passing**: A racer passes another if they start a move behind them and end that move ahead of them.
